# plus

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

~~~
R=X plus Y
~~~

X and Y must be numeric. R is the sum of X and Y. R is numeric. plus may also be written as '+'.

## Examples

~~~
      2 plus 2
┌────┐
│4   │
└Int8┘
      3 + 5
┌────┐
│8   │
└Int8┘
      3 + 4 5
┌────┐
↓7   │
│8   │
└Int8┘
~~~
