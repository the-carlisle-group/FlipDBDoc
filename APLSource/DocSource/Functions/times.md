# times

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

~~~
R=X times Y
~~~

X and Y must be numeric. R is the product of X and Y.
times may also be written as `*`.

## Examples

~~~
      5 times 3
┌────┐
│15  │
└Int8┘
      5 * 1 2 3
┌────┐
↓5   │
│10  │
│15  │
└Int8┘
      1 2 3 * 4 5 6
┌────┐
↓4   │
│10  │
│18  │
└Int8┘
~~~
