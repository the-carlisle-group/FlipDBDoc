# lcm

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

Lowest common multiple.{.purpose}

~~~
R=X lcm Y
~~~

X and Y must both be numeric. R is numeric.  R is the lowest common multiple of X and Y.

## Examples

~~~
      15 1 2 7 lcm 35 1 4 0
┌────┐
↓105 │
│  1 │
│  4 │
│  0 │
└Int8┘
~~~
