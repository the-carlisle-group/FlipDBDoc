# covariance

Type:{.prefix}

Aggregate{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Statistical{.info}

⍎⍎#.FlipDB.flipdb.TamStatInt.HelpMsg 0 ⍎⍎

~~~
R=X covariance Y
~~~

* X and Y must be numeric and conformable.
* R is the covariance of X and Y.

See also: →[variance], →[standardDeviation]

## Examples

~~~
      1 3 2 1 3 covariance 14 24 18 17 27
┌─────┐
│5    │
└Float┘
~~~
