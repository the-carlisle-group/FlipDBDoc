# gcd

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

Greatest common divisor.{.purpose}

~~~
R=X gcd Y
~~~

X and Y must both be numeric. R is numeric. R is the greatest common divisor of X and Y.

## Examples

~~~
      15 1 2 7 gcd 35 1 4 0
┌────┐
↓5   │
│1   │
│2   │
│7   │
└Int8┘
~~~
