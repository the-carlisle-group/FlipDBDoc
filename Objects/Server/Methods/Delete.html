Delete Method

Applies to:{.prefix}

→[##.##.Server]{.info}

The Delete method deletes a resource from the path portion of its URL. The schema and domain should not be included in the argument.

The argument is composed of 1 item:

- -

The result is composed of 1 item:

- -

For Example:

               s.Exists '/Databases/SandP'
┌───────┐
│1      │
└Boolean┘
       s.Delete '/Databases/SandP'
┌───────┐
│0      │
└Boolean┘
       s.Exists '/Databases/SandP'
┌───────┐
│0      │
└Boolean┘
