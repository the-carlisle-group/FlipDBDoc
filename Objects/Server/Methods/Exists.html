Exists Method

Applies to:{.prefix}

→[##.##.Server]{.info}

The Exists method verifies the existance of a resource from the path portion of its URL. The schema and domain should not be included in the argument.

The argument is composed of 1 item:

- -

The result is composed of 1 item:

- -
