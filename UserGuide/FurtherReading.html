Further Reading

The PCRE open source library has concise, very well written documentation freely available at www.pcre.org.

Specifically there is a syntax summary page:

http://www.pcre.org/current/doc/html/pcre2syntax.html

And a regular expressions details page:

http://www.pcre.org/current/doc/html/pcre2pattern.html

The most comprehensive book is Mastering Regular Expressions by Jeffery E.F. Friedl, now in its 3rd edition.
