identical

Type:{.prefix}

Aggregate{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Logical{.info}

Identical; all items identical{.purpose}

        R=identical X

X may be of any class. R is boolean; 1 if all items of X are identical; 0 if not.

Examples

                identical 1 1 1 1
┌───────┐
│1      │
└Boolean┘
        identical 'A,B,A'
┌───────┐
│0      │
└Boolean┘
       identical 5
┌───────┐
│1      │
└Boolean┘
