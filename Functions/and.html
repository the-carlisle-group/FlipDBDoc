and

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Logical{.info}

Logical and, both{.purpose}

        R=X and Y

X and Y must both be boolean. R is Boolean being 1 if both X & Y are 1.

See also: →[all]

Examples

              B3 = 0 0 1 1
      B5 = 0 1 0 1
      B3 B5 (B3 and B5)

 │1      │  │0      │  │0      │
 │1      │  │1      │  │1      │
