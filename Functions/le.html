le

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Relational{.info}

Less than or equal to.{.purpose}

        R=X le Y

X and Y must be numeric. R is boolean and is 1 if X is less than or equal to Y. This function may also be written as <=.

Examples

              a=1 2 3 4 5
      b=5 4 3 2 1
      a le b
