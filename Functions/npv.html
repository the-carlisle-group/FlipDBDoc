Net PresentValue{.purpose}

        R=X npv Y

is the discount rate in decimal form. * Y is a series of cash fl

R is the net present value. Net present Value is the total present value of all cash flows at the discount rate specified in the left argument. A positive net present value is a good investment, a negative net present value is a bad investment.
