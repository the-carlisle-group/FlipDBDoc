        R=kurtosis X

is numeric. * R is the (excess) kurtosis o

Kurtosis is the fourth moment about the mean. If the data are approximately normal, the excess kurtosis is close to zero. Positive skewness for symmetric distributions indicates that the data are more peaked about the center and have thicker tails than the normal distribution. For example, the Student t Distribution has positive kurtosis but decreases as the number of degrees of freedom increases.
