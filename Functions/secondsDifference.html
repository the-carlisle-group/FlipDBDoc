secondsDifference

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Date/Time{.info}

Seconds difference between two datetimes.{.purpose}

        R=X secondsDifference Y

X and Y must be DateTime or Date. R is Integer. R is the number of seconds between the datetimes in X and Y. A date is treated as a datetime with a zero time element. R will be negative where Y is greater than X. Where either X or Y is zero (null), R is zero.

See also: →[minutesDifference], →[hoursDifference], →[daysDifference], →[monthsDifference], →[yearsDifference]
