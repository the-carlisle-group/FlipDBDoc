nonZeroLow

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

lower, lesser, non-zero{.purpose}

        R=X nonZeroLow Y

X and Y must both be numeric. R is the lower value of X and Y unless the lower is zero when the other value is taken. R is numeric.

See also: →[minimum], →[min]

Examples

              0 10 20 nonZeroLow 1 2 3
