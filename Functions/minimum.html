minimum

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

lower, lesser{.purpose}

        R=X minimum Y

X and Y must both be numeric. R is the smaller value of X and Y. R is numeric.

See also: →[min], →[nonZeroLow], →[maximum]

Examples

              1 2 3 minimum 3 2 1
