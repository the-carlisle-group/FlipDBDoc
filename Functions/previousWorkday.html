previousWorkday

Applies to:{.prefix}

Date{.info}

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Date/Time{.info}

Find the previous working day{.purpose}

        R=[X] previousWorkday Y

Y is Date. R is also Date having identical structure to Y and represents the previous working day before each date in Y. Dates in R exclude weekends or holidays.

X is an optional scalar or simple Char and represents one or more country codes; the default is 'US'. Dates in R exclude weekends or holidays in any country included in X.

See also: →[addDays], →[nextWorkday]

Examples

              D='2010-12-25'addDays integers 10
      P=previousWorkday D
      D(weekday D)P(weekday P)
