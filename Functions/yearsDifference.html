yearsDifference

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Date/Time{.info}

Years difference between two dates.{.purpose}

        R=X yearsDifference Y

X and Y must be Date or DateTime. R is Integer. R is the number of years between the starts of the years in X and Y. R will be negative if Y is greater than X. If either X or Y is zero (null), R is zero.

See also: →[daysDifference], →[monthsDifference]

Examples

              '2012-10-11' yearsDifference '1964-7-24'
