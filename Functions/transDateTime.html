transDateTime

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Date/Time{.info}

        R=transDateTime X

X must be the system column TWID, or a virtual column pointing to the TWID column of another table. R is the DateTime associated with the corresponding transaction id number.

See also: →[transUsername]

Samples

              d=Scripting.Server.OpenDatabase 'SandP'
      t=d.GetTable 'S'
      t.Evaluate 'transDateTime TWID'
