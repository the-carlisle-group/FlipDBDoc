proportion

Type:{.prefix}

Aggregate{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Statistical{.info}

Calculates the sample proportion{.purpose}

        R=proportion X

X must be boolean. R is Float. 0<=R<=1.

Examples

              proportion 1 1 0 1 1 1 0 1 1
┌────────────┐
│0.7777777778│
└Float───────┘
      proportion roll 100 replicate 2
┌─────┐
│0.52 │
└Float┘
