rotateChar

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Text{.info}

Rotates the characters in a string.{.purpose}

        R=X rotateChar Y

Y is Char. X is numeric and must be scalar or conform in length to Y. R is Y with the characters rotated according to X.

Examples

              2 rotateChar 'Paul'
