ge

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Relational{.info}

Greater than or equal to.{.purpose}

        R=X ge Y

X and Y must be numeric. R is boolean and is 1 if X is greater than or equal to Y. This function may also be written as >= .

Examples

              3 ge 1 2 3 4 5 6
