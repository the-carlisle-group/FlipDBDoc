deal

Type:{.prefix}

Structural{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Misc{.info}

Produce a set of "random" numbers.{.purpose}

        R=X deal Y

X and Y are both Integer scalars. R is X numbers selected "randomly" from the first Y non-negative integers and is simple. X must be non-negative and less than or equal to Y.

Examples:

              (1 deal 5)(2 deal 5)(3 deal 5)(4 deal 5)(5 deal 5)
