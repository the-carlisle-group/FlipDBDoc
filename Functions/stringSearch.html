stringSearch

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Relational,Text{.info}

Search for strings in a char column.{.purpose}

        R=X stringSearch Y

X and Y must be Char. Y is a scalar or simple. R is boolean.

R will be 1 where rows of X contain one or more of the strings in Y as a substring. The search is case-insensitive. Commas required in literal arguments will require escaping as: "this, string"

Examples

              (M) (M stringSearch 'uar,emb') (M stringSearch 'R')
