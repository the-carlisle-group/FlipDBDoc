without

Type:{.prefix}

{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

{.info}

Selection{.purpose}

        R=X without Y

X and Y must be compatible types. R has type and value of X with elements that also occur anywhere in Y removed. The structure of R is also that of X excepting that Scalar X is converted to a one or zero item Simple.

Examples
