ne

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Relational{.info}

Not equal to.{.purpose}

        R=X ne Y

X and Y must be conforming types. R is boolean. R is 1 if X is not equal to Y. ne may also be written as '<>'.

Examples

              1 ne 0 1 2
┌───────┐
↓1      │
│0      │
│1      │
└Boolean┘
      'Tom' <> 'Jon,Tom,Tim,Tom'
┌───────┐
↓1      │
│0      │
│1      │
│0      │
└Boolean┘
