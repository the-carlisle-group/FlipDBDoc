squareRoot

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

Calculates the positive square root.{.purpose}

        R=squareRoot X

X must be greater than or equal to zero.

R is the positive square root. R is Float

Examples

              squareRoot 3 16
┌──────────────────┐
↓1.7320508075688772│
│4                 │
└Float─────────────┘
