futureValue

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Financial{.info}

        R=futureValue I N P [PV[A]]

is the periodic interest rate expressed in decimal form. * N is the total number of payment periods. Numeric. * P is the fixed payment made each period. Numeric. * PV is the present value. Numeric (Optional). * A is Boolean. 0=Arrears 1=Advanced (Option

R is the future value (remaining balance). R is Float. The futureValue function follows the convention that incoming and outgoing payments are indicated by positive and negative signs respectively.

PV and A are optional, but you cannot input A without PV.

See also: →[mortgageBalance], →[presentValue]

Examples

              futureValue 0.007 48 -350
