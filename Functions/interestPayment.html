interestPayment

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Financial{.info}

        R=interestPayment I A N PV [FV]

is a periodic rate expressed as a decimal. * A is age (the number of periods in the future). Numeric. * N is the term of the loan. Numeric. * PV is the present value. Numeric. * FV is the future value. Nume

R is the interest payable in period A. R is Float. Total payment = Principal payment + interest payment.

See also: →[principalPayment], →[payment]

Examples

              interestPayment .07 10 15 20000

│-732.67660629277│
└Float───────────┘
      interestPayment (9.5 divide 1200) 3 360 100000
