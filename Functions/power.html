power

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

        R=[X] power Y

X and Y must be numeric. R is X raised to the exponent Y. R is numeric. If X is omitted, it defaults to the numerical contant e (2.7182818...) and power is the exponential function.

Examples
