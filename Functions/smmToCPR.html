smmToCPR

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Financial{.info}

Convert SMM (single monthly mortality) to CPR (constant prepayment rate){.purpose}

        R=smmToCPR Y

Y is a SMM value, R is CPR.

This function is equivalent to:

           R = 100 times 1 - (1 - Y) power 12

Examples ~~~ A = .01 .002 .003 smmToCPR A ┌────────────────┐ ↓11.361512828387 │ │ 2.3737752105285│ │ 3.5411900096784│ └Float───────────┘ 100 times 1 - (1 -A) power 12 ┌────────────────┐ ↓11.361512828387 │ │ 2.3737752105285│ │ 3.5411900096784│ └Float───────────┘ ~~~
