nTile

Type:{.prefix}

Hybrid{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Partitions a column into groups. {.purpose}

        R=X nTile Y

Y is any column. X is a postive integer. R is Integer from 0 to X-1. R partitions Y into X equal sized groups, if possible. If the length of Y is not a perfect a perfect multiple of X, the groups at the beginning will be 1 larger as necessary.
