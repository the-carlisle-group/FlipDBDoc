minus

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

        R=X minus Y

X and Y must be numeric. R is the difference between X and Y. R is numeric. This function may also be written symbolically as '-'.

Note that the minus symbol is also used to denote a negative number, and thus when used as a function, it must be followed by a space.

Examples
