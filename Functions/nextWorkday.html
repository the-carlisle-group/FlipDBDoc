nextWorkday

Applies to:{.prefix}

Date{.info}

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Date/Time{.info}

Find the next working day{.purpose}

        R=[X] nextWorkday Y

Y is Date. R is also Date having identical structure to Y and represents the next working day after each date in Y. Dates in R exclude weekends or holidays.

X is an optional scalar or simple Char and represents one or more country codes; the default is 'US'. Dates in R exclude weekends or holidays in any country included in X.

See also: →[ .addDays],→[ .previousWorkday]

Examples

              D='2010-12-23'addDays integers 10
      N=nextWorkday D
      D(weekday D)N(weekday N)
