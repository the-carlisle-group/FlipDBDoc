residue

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

        R=X residue Y

X and Y must be numeric. R is the remainder of Y divided by X.

For positive arguments, R is the remainder when Y is divided by X. If X is 0, R is Y. For other argument values, R is Y minus N times X where N is some integer such that R lies between 0 and X, but is not equal to X.

Examples

              3 3 -3 -3 residue -5 5 -4 4
