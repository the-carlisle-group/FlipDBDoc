frequency

Type:{.prefix}

Structural{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Statistical{.info}

        R=[Y] frequency X

X is any column. Y is an optional list of values. If Y is omitted, R is the frequency of the unique items in X, returned in the order of appearance in X. If Y is provided, R is corresponding frequency of items in X.

Examples: ~~~ A='NY,NY,NJ,CT,CT,NJ,CT,NY,NY' frequency A ┌────┐ ↓4 │ │2 │ │3 │ └Int8┘ uniqueValues A ┌───────┐ ↓NY │ │NJ │ │CT │ └Char(2)┘ 'CT,MA,NY' frequency A ┌────┐ ↓3 │ │0 │ │4 │ └Int8┘ 'NY' frequency A ┌────┐ │4 │ └Int8┘ sum A in 'NY' ┌────┐ │4 │ └Int8┘ ~~~
