divide

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

        R=X divide Y

X and Y must be numeric. R is X divided by Y. R is Float.

The result of any number divided by 0 is 0. The divide function may also be written as ' / '.

Examples

              a=1 2 3
      b=0 1 -2
      a b
