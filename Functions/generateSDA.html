generateSDA

Type:{.prefix}

Structural{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Financial{.info}

Generate "Standard Default Assumption" curve.{.purpose}

        R=[Y] generateSDA X

X is a percentage value. Y is optional loan age. R is the age-adjusted X percent of SDA.
