factorial

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Arithmetic{.info}

        R=factorial X

Y must be numeric excluding negative integers. R is numeric.

R is the product of the first Y integers for positive integer values of Y. For non-integral values of Y, Y is equivalent to the gamma function of Y+1.

Examples

│  2                │
