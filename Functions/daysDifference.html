daysDifference

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Date/Time{.info}

Days difference between two dates.{.purpose}

        R=X daysDifference Y

X and Y must be Date or DateTime. R is Integer. R is the number of days between the starts of the days in X and Y. R will be negative if Y is greater than X. If either X or Y is zero (null), R is zero.

See also: →[monthsDifference], →[yearsDifference]

Examples

              d='2012-01-01,2011-07-24,0000-00-00'
      d

↓2012-01-01│
