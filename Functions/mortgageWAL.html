Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Weighted average life of a mortgage in years.{.purpose}

        R=mortgageWAL I N [C]

is the annualized interest rate expressed as a percentage. Numeric. * N is the number of months remaining in the mortgage. Numeric. * C is the constant prepayment rate expressed as a percentage (Optional, default=0). Nume

R is the average time to return a principal in years. R is Float.

All inputs should be positive. Unexpected values may occur with negative numbers.

See also: →[averageLife]

Examples

              mortgageWAL 9.5 360
