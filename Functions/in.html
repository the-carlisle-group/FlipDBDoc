in

Type:{.prefix}

Scalar{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Relational{.info}

Membership{.purpose}

        R=X in Y

X and Y must be compatible types. R is boolean being 1 for each element in X that is also an element of Y or of any of its partitions and 0 otherwise.

Examples

              a='NY,NY,CA,NJ,PA'
      a

↓NY     │

│1      │

└Boolean┘
