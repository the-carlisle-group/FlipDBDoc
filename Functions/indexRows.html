indexRows

Type:{.prefix}

Table{.info}

Categories:{.prefix}

Selection{.info}

        R=X indexRows Y

X must be a DataTable or a Table, and Y must be integer. R is a DataTable formed by selecting the rows in X specified by the indices in Y. The index-origin is zero. Negative indices indicate rows from the bottom of the table.

Examples:

              DT=('One' (1 2 3 4 5)) ('Two' ('A,B,C,D,E'))

 └Int8┘  └Char(1)┘
── 5 rows by 2 columns
